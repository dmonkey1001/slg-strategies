Q&A Highlights

How are Universities involved?
Working groups meet in January. The goal is to connect industry-defined challenges with the specific academic experts best suited to solve them.
How do large companies engage?
Engagement is segmented by division. Operations, Executive Leadership, and R&D divisions often have different entry points and needs within the Hub ecosystem.
What are the success metrics?
Metrics are twofold: Program-specific (count of startups, pilots launched, capital raised) and broader economic indicators (regional job growth, adoption rates).
