Political & Executive Landscape

Understanding the key decision-makers and the strategic "North Star" directives shaping IT investment.

The Executive
Governor Roy Cooper

Prioritizes "Digital Equity" and workforce development. Strong supporter of centralized IT governance to improve efficiency.

The Technology Lead
State CIO (NCDIT)

Cabinet-level position. Focuses on "Whole-of-State" cybersecurity and cloud migration (NC Cloud). Controls most agency spend.

The Fiscal Lead
State Budget Director

Oversees OSBM. Focuses on sustainability of IT projects post-federal stimulus. Strict ROI requirements.
